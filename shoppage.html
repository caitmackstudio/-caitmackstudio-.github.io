All prints are produced locally in St Werburghs, Bristol by 212 Productions - an independent specialist in high quality artists' prints.

Prints are sold on a 'pay what you can' basis. There are three price options:

1. Pay more - if you are able to add a little extra to support my work

2. Standard price

3. Pay less - if you can't afford the standard price

Payments are managed with Paypal. Don't have Paypal? No worries! Send your order to caitmack.studio@gmail.com and we can find another way.
